Jobs Description

Looking for an experienced candidate to join us on an immediate basis as a Documentation Officer.You will be responsible for Advising the clients of their lacking documents and following up for the completion of their requirements and With proven experience in assisting foreign nationals in Bureau of Immigration for hearing interviews, With good communication skills both in oral and written, Able to process payments, liquidations and cost budgeting of all visa expenses and Knowledge on booking flights, Hotel and Tour Packages is a plus but not required

Responsibilities

The duties and responsibilities of a Documentation Officer is they are the one who is responsible for Checking the approved visas and other travel documents for accuracy,Ensuring that all development and information related to travel documents processing is relayed to your immediate superior.


In addition to responsibilities that are unique to our company, the following are some of the more common responsibilities for Documentation Officer:

  • Releases the travel documents to the Clients or Travel Consultants in a timely manner

  • Encash the Cash Advance and pay the necessary fees in filing of visas and other travel documents

  • Ensure that all development and information related to travel documents processing is relayed to your immediate superior.

  • Constantly updating the visa processing procedures and fees

  • Assure proper documentation and filing of travel and immigration documents like Passport and VISA

  • Assist clients in securing appointments and giving detailed instructions for personal appearance

  • Perform clerical duties such as filing, photocopying and scanning of documents
